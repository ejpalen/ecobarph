Ecobar Moisture Retention Gentle Face Bar

₱190.00

Description:

Ecobar PH Moisture Retention Gentle Face Bar is made of syndet (synthetic detergent) that has a pH of around 5 to 6 which is very similar to the pH of the skin.

It gently washes away dirt and does not strip off the natural barrier leaving skin soft, moisturized, and less irritated compared to soap.

This bar is formulated with hyaluronic acid, a naturally occurring substance found in the skin that can increase hydration. It penetrates easily making the skin more elastic and reducing fine lines. Suitable for dry and sensitive skin.

Ecobar PH’s line of functional bars does not have strong and irritating ingredients that can strip natural oils and moisture from the scalp and hair. Ecobar PH omitted sulfates, silicones, and synthetic dyes, which can cause irritation and build-up, then replaced with milder and safer ingredients suitable for sensitive scalp and hair. This solid bar can outlast liquid products, does not require plastic packaging, and is convenient to use for traveling and outdoor activities.

Ingredients:

Sodium Cocoyl Isethionate, Stearic Acid, Cetyl Alcohol, Wheat flour, Aqua (and) Sodium Lauroyl Methyl Isethionate (and) Cocamidopropyl Betaine (and) Sodium Methyl Oleoyl Taurate (and) Lauryl Glucoside (and) Coco-Glucoside, Hyaluronic Acid, Phenoxyethanol (and) Ethylhexylglycerin (and) Octenidine HCL, Etidronic Acid

Suggested Usage:

Lather with hands, then apply directly onto face.
