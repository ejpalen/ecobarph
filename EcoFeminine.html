Description:

A new way to take care of the skin and the intimate area is to incorporate prebiotics into your daily routine. Prebiotics is a series of glucose units and oligosaccharide that is obtained from the fermentation of plant substrates.

These plant substrates feed the friendly microorganisms on our skin and stimulate the production of antimicrobial peptides, thus increasing immunity against harmful bacteria.

By balancing the skin microbiome, it can help prevent dryness, itchiness, and infection on the skin and intimate area. Overall, it maintains a healthy bacterial balance on the skin.

Ecobar PH eco+biome Prebiotic Feminine Bar contains Alpha-glucan oligosaccharides from the fermentation of natural sugars that act as food for beneficial bacteria on the skin. The beneficial bacteria produce antimicrobial peptides that inhibit the growth of pathogenic bacteria. By balancing the skin microbiome, it can help prevent dryness, itchiness, and infection in the intimate area.

Ingredients:

Sodium Cocoyl Isethionate, Stearic Acid, Cetyl Alcohol, Wheat flour, Aqua (and) Sodium Lauroyl Methyl Isethionate (and) Cocamidopropyl Betaine (and) Sodium Methyl Oleoyl Taurate (and) Lauryl Glucoside (and) Coco-Glucoside, Niacinamide, Alpha-glucan Oligosaccharide, Illite clay, Phenoxyethanol (and) Ethylhexylglycerin (and) Octenidine HCL, Etidronic Acid

Suggested Usage:

Lather with hands then applies onto the intimate area. Rinse with water..

NOTE: Keep the product in a dry place after every use. Avoid prolonged contact with water. Store at room temperature. For external use only. Rinse with plenty of water in case of eye contact.
