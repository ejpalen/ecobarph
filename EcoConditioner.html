Ecobar Co-Wash Conditioner Bar

₱220.00

Description:

This cleansing bar gently removes dirt and impurities while helping define curls. Infused with nourishing coconut oil, shea butter, and vitamin E, Co-Wash Conditioner Bar moisturizes and protects delicate curls from frizz. Use it between wash days with our range of sulfate-free shampoo bars for optimum results.

Ecobar PH’s line of functional bars does not have strong and irritating ingredients that can strip natural oils and moisture from the scalp and hair. Ecobar PH omitted sulfates, silicones, and synthetic dyes, which can cause irritation and build-up, then replaced with milder and safer ingredients suitable for sensitive scalp and hair. This solid bar can outlast liquid products, does not require plastic packaging, and is convenient to use for traveling and outdoor activities.

Ingredients:

Cetearyl Alcohol (and) Brassicamidopropyl Dimethylamine (and) Aspartic Acid (and) EDTA, Cetyl Alcohol, Stearic Acid, Behentrimonium Methosulfate (and) Isopropyl Alcohol, Polyquaternium 116, PEG-2, Cocos nucifera oil, Butyrospermum parkii butter, Tocopheryl Acetate, Fragrance, C.I. 77019

Suggested Usage:

• Massage into scalp and hair. The emulsification action removes dirt and excess oil.
• Rinse thoroughly.
• Use it between wash days with Ecobar's range of sulfate-free shampoo bars for optimum results.
