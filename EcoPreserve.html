Ecobar Color Preserve Shampoo Bar

₱170.00

Description:

Introducing Ecobar PH's Color Preserve Shampoo Bar for daily use. This is a great bar designed to complement the Color Preserve Conditioner Bar.

The properties of grapeseed and jojoba oil deeply moisturize scalp and hair and help retain its color. These natural oils also contain vitamins, minerals, and amino acids which help repair damaged cuticles preserving the color.

Ecobar PH’s line of functional bars does not have strong and irritating ingredients that can strip natural oils and moisture from the scalp and hair. Ecobar PH omitted sulfates, silicones, and synthetic dyes, which can cause irritation and build-up, then replaced with milder and safer ingredients suitable for sensitive scalp and hair. This solid bar can outlast its liquid counterpart, does not require plastic packaging, and is convenient to use for traveling and outdoor activities.

Ingredients:

Sodium Cocoyl Isethionate, Triticum Vulgare (Wheat) flour, Cetyl Alcohol, Stearic Acid, Decyl Glucoside, Capryloyl/Caproyl Methyl Glucamide (and) Lauroyl/Myristoyl Methyl Glucamide, Brassicamidopropyl Dimethylamine, PEG-2 Dimeadowfoamamidoethylmonium Methosulfate, Jojoba Oil, Grapeseed oil, Tocopheryl Acetate, Phenoxyethanol (and) Ethylhexylglycerin (and) Octenidine HCL, Citric Acid, Etidronic Acid, C.I. 42090, 77492

Suggested Usage:

Rub directly onto hair and scalp. Lather then rinse.
