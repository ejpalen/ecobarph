Ecobar Repair and Nourish Conditioner Bar

₱220.00

Description:

This nourishing bar is infused with natural oils rich in vitamins, proteins, and amino acids that instantly repair dry and damaged hair. This vitamin-rich formula replenishes, detangles, and restores luster without creating build-up.

Free of sulfates, parabens, silicones, and synthetic dyes.

Ecobar PH’s line of functional bars does not have strong and irritating ingredients that can strip natural oils and moisture from the scalp and hair. Ecobar PH omitted sulfates, silicones, and synthetic dyes, which can cause irritation and build-up, then replaced with milder and safer ingredients suitable for sensitive scalp and hair. This solid bar can outlast its liquid counterpart, does not require plastic packaging, and is convenient to use for traveling and outdoor activities.

Ingredients:

Cetearyl Alcohol (and) Brassicamidopropyl Dimethylamine (and) Aspartic Acid (and) EDTA, Cetyl Alcohol, Stearic Acid, Polyquaternium 116, Shea Butter, Coconut Oil, Sunflower Oil, Almond Oil, Fragrance

Suggested Usage:

Apply directly on hair and scalp.
