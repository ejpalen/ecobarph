About Ecobar

Ecobar is a Filipino-owned company. We offer sustainable, eco-friendly and quality products that are 100% natural. The best thing in Ecobar is that we use locally-sourced ingredients for our products. In Ecobar, we strive for the best and the good for our environment.

Ecobar's goal is to inspire our consumers to be aware of the products they use without sacrificing too much of their lifestyle. We aim to reduce our usage of plastics in your orders; through shredded papers and sticker labels as cushions, wrapping the product with donated magazines, and providing plastic-free delivery.

Ecobar is not only sustainable, but also accessible. Caring for our environment doesn’t have to be costly. That is why we produce quality and pro-environment products. We also make sure that our products are budget-friendly and affordable.

Here's to continuously finding ways to make homegrown and quality products created by Filipinos be accessible to Filipinos. High five!
