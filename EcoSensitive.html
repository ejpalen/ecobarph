Ecobar Sensitive Shampoo Bar

₱170.00

Description:

Fragrance-free, soothing effect on the scalp. This synergy of 9 natural extracts (Aloe Vera, Centella, Chamomile, Japanese Knotweed, Licorice, Rosemary, Seaweed, Skullcap, and Tea) provides a strong anti-irritation effect and soothes itchiness.

Suitable for sensitive scalp and hypersensitive skin conditions.

Ecobar PH’s line of functional bars does not have strong and irritating ingredients that can strip natural oils and moisture from the scalp and hair. Ecobar PH omitted sulfates, silicones, and synthetic dyes, which can cause irritation and build-up, then replaced with milder and safer ingredients suitable for sensitive scalp and hair. This solid bar can outlast its liquid counterpart, does not require plastic packaging, and is convenient to use for traveling and outdoor activities.

Ingredients:

Sodium Cocoyl Isethionate, Triticum Vulgare (Wheat) flour, Cetyl Alcohol, Stearic Acid, Decyl Glucoside, Cetearyl Alcohol (and) Brassicamidopropyl Dimethylamine (and) Aspartic Acid (and) EDTA, Polyester 11, Centella extract, Tea extract, Chamomile extract, Japanese Knotweed extract, Rosemary extract, Aloe Vera extract, Phenoxyethanol (and) Ethylhexylglycerin (and) Octenidine HCL, Citric Acid, Etidronic Acid.

Suggested Usage:

Rub directly onto hair and scalp. Lather then rinse.
